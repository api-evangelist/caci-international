# CACI International (caci-international)
CACI International provides information technology, professional services, and managed solutions to U.S. federal government agencies in the defense, intelligence, homeland security, and civilian markets. The company delivers expertise and technology across C3I, cyber, digital solutions, enterprise IT, mission support, space, and spectrum superiority domains.
